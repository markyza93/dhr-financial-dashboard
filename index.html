Expense Structure

Recognition basis — mixed, by source. DHR Inc Expenses and 2026 DHR EOOD Expenses are invoice-based — costs recorded in the month the invoice is dated. Unicredit Bulbank is cash-based — recorded when the payment clears the account. Note: BG Operations payroll follows the Bulgarian arrears system (salaries for month N paid in N+1, so Feb reflects two months of payroll). US tax payments are recorded in the month paid regardless of the tax period they cover.

Margin = Net Profit ÷ Revenue — the share of each revenue dollar retained after all costs. Add-backs = CapEx (land acquisitions only, capitalised not expensed) + Taxes paid in the month — items that reduce reported profit but are not recurring operational costs. EBITDA Proxy = Profit + Add-backs — a simplified proxy for recurring operating cash generation. EBITDA Proxy Margin = EBITDA Proxy ÷ Revenue, which normalises across months (e.g. Mar margin of −101% is distorted by a one-time $467K land purchase; EBITDA Proxy Margin of −38% strips that out).
