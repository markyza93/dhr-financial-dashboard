Expense Structure

Recognition basis — mixed, by source. DHR Inc Expenses and 2026 DHR EOOD Expenses are invoice-based — costs recorded in the month the invoice is dated. Unicredit Bulbank is cash-based — recorded when the payment clears the account. Note: BG Operations payroll follows the Bulgarian arrears system (salaries for month N paid in N+1, so Feb reflects two months of payroll). US tax payments are recorded in the month paid regardless of the tax period they cover.
